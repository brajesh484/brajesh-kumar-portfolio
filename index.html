    Itvaar Ka Ek Din
    by Arahaan

    "Itvaar Ka Ek Din" is a collection of short stories that captures the subtle emotional textures of ordinary days. Each story finds grace, tension, humor, or revelation in moments most people overlook.

    The book reflects a deep belief that the most profound human experiences often hide in the simplest routines — a Sunday morning, a delayed train, a quiet conversation at dusk.
